OVERVIEW

We deliver Medicaid-qualified clients seeking in-home care services to Healthcare Agencies seeking eligble clients.
All referrals are pre-screened & verified prior to intake.

REFERRALS

Consulting HQ facilitates verified, intake-qualified Medicaid client referrals for in-home care agencies.

Referral types may include 24-hour or non-24-hour care, with or without skilled providers, based on agency criteria already provided to Consulting HQ.

Donation Structure (General Example)

Donation obligations are determined by the scope of care associated with each accepted referral.

Example:
• $1,000 per referral
• $500 due within 14 days of referral release
• $500 due within 14 additional days

Initial Test Batch Example

• 4 referrals released upon agreement execution
• Total donation obligation: $4,000
• $2,000 due within 14 days
• $2,000 due within 14 additional days

Test batches are limited and issued to confirm operational alignment prior to expansion.

Referral Agreement Execution

Participation in the Consulting HQ referral program requires execution of the official Referral Agreement.

No client information will be released unless and until the agreement has been reviewed and signed.

Referral Donation Method

Referral donations are remitted by certified check made payable to:

Marie-Charles Consulting LLC

Donation timing and amount are governed by the selected referral type and participation agreement.
