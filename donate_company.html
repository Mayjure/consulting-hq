Donation Structure

Donation obligations are determined by the selected referral type and quantity accepted.

24-Hour Care with Skilled Provider — $1,000 per referral

• $500 due within 14 days of referral acceptance
• $500 due within 14 additional days

24-Hour Care without Skilled Provider — $600 per referral

• $300 due within 14 days of referral acceptance
• $300 due within 14 additional days

Less Than 24-Hour Care with Skilled Provider — up to $500 per referral

• Donation amount determined by care scope
• Due within 14 days of referral acceptance

Less Than 24-Hour Care without Skilled Provider — up to $500 per referral

• Donation amount determined by care scope
• Due within 14 days of referral acceptance

Initial Test Batch Example

• 4 referrals @ $1,000 each = $4,000 total
• $2,000 due within 14 days
• $2,000 due within 14 additional days

Test batches may be limited and are subject to completion prior to expansion.
