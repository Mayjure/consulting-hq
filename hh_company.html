Referral Donation Structure

Referral donations are required from the receiving Home Health Care Company prior to the release of any client information.

The referral donation is $1,000 per client, paid in two installments:

  • $500 due within 14 days of referral acceptance
  • $500 due within 14 additional days

Referral Release Policy

Client information is released only after the initial donation installment is received.

Initial test period is limited to two (2) clients. Additional referrals are released in controlled batches.

Failure to complete the second installment may result in suspension of future referrals.
