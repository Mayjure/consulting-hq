What Is a Donation?

A donation is a voluntary transfer of value made without expectation of goods, services, wages, or compensation in return.

Donations are provided to support operations, education, administration, and facilitation efforts conducted by Consulting HQ.

Donations are not classified as fees, wages, payments, revenue, or consideration for services.

Purpose of Donations

Donations allow Consulting HQ to operate independently while providing educational resources, referral facilitation, and administrative coordination.

This structure ensures neutrality, flexibility, and ethical separation between contributors and outcomes.

Home Health Referral Donation

Participating Home Health Care Companies are required to submit a $1,000 donation per qualified referral pursuant to agreement.

Clients do not submit donations.

Private Consulting Donation

Private consulting services require a $500 per hour donation prior to scheduling or document preparation.

Donation readiness is required before intake.
